## Packages
framer-motion | Complex animations for the news ticker and page transitions
lucide-react | Beautiful icons for the UI
date-fns | Date formatting for news items
clsx | Utility for constructing className strings conditionally
tailwind-merge | Utility for merging Tailwind classes safely

## Notes
- News data is fetched from `/api/news`
- Settings are persisted in `localStorage`
- "Read Later" is persisted in `localStorage`
- Ticker animation needs to handle both horizontal and vertical directions
